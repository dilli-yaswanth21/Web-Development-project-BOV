Burj Khalifa

The Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world, reaching a height of 828 meters (2,717 feet). It is located in Dubai, United Arab Emirates and is a popular tourist attraction. The building features a mix of residential, commercial, and hotel spaces, as well as observation decks that offer stunning views of the city.The Burj Khalifa is also known for its spectacular light shows and fireworks that illuminate the night sky on special occasions.
